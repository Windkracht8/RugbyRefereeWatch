Welcome
Here are a few tips to get you familiar with this app.

New stuff
Looking for penalty kick? Check under foul play.

Corrections
During a game press the back button to open the correction screen. On this screen you can tab the event you want to undo.

Kick off
Before the game starts you can record which team will kick off by tapping the home or away team score.

Battery
Screen on is a very useful setting to keep the screen on during a game, but this will use more battery. Consider switching off Wifi, GPS and Bluetooth during a game, this app does not use these.

Storage
Matches will be stored on the watch for 14 days, use the companion app on your phone to download them.

Get help
From the settings you can open this screen. Find the support contacts in the App Store for more help.
